## Components to Use

- Microcontroller
  - Criterion
    - Have enough power to receive data from computer, process it and then display images on OLED
    - Be easy enough to use and be small enough for my form factor
  - Options
    - Raspberry Pi RP2040
      - Pros
        - Faster
        - More Memory
        - Used by most people
      - Cons
        - Still simple, just less than the ATmega32U4
    - ATmega32U4 (Arduino Pro Micro)
      - Pros
        - Simple
      - Cons
        - Performance
- Switches
  - Criterion
    - Durability
    - Consistency
    - Feel
  - Cherry MX Red
    - Both RED & BLack are equal and very good in durability and consistency
    - Light
    - Quiet
    - Smooth
  - Cherry MX Black
    - Both RED & BLack are equal and very good in durability and consistency
    - Smooth 
    - Heavy
    - When lubricated - buttery(big priority)

- Rotary Encoder
  - Criterion
    - Durable
    - Smooth
    - Consistent
  - Some I found online
    - Everything pointed to EC-11 type encoders - a bit about them
      - They are budget-friendly
      - 360 degree endless rotations
      - Compact Size

What I chose

- RP2040 - KB2040
- Cherry MX Black
- EC11 Rotary Encoder
